Font Pairings
  1. Complement: harmonize, without being too similar or too different.
  2. Superfamilies: within the same superfamily is safe.
  3. Contrast is King: combining serif with sans serif is a well known combination.
  4. Only One Boss: by varying the size, weight and color.
